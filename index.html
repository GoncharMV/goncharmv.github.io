About me

Hello, I’m Rita. And I’m gonna be King of the Pirates!

I have many projects I’ve been developing during my year of study Java, and after as a solo developer. However, the project I am most proud of is the Fandom Guess application, which I developed myself with help of a designer and a translator. You can find my app on Google Play or read more about its development process on its web page using the link above. In addition to that, I’ve been trying myself in game design; I have created three completed games using the Godot engine and currently have another one in development.

I can call myself an experienced team-player. In my recent project, Fandom Guess, besides developing the app singlehandedly I was responsible for the organisation. I set priorities, handed out assignments, and coordinated the work of my team, as well as organizing and supervising the work of 20 testers. Another example is a group project I had with 4 other students during my studies; we worked on the business logic of the project, developed new features and had hands-on experience. Even back in my student years I was a regular participant of creative team projects; with teams up to 20 people—each proficient in different areas—I was responsible for communication between players and for presentation of our works, using HTML and CSS.

I am looking for work mostly as a Java developer; however, I am open to other opportunities.
